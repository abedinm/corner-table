About

Where neighbors become regulars.

Corner Table opened as a small idea between three friends who wanted to cook the kind of food they'd actually want to eat after a long day — unfussy, generous, made with what's in season at the farmers' market down the road.

Seven years later we're still here, still cooking from a hand-written menu that changes when the produce does, and still pouring the same honest wines we started with.
